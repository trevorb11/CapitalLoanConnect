Here is the complete, step-by-step guide to implementing the **Full Funding Application Workflow** in your Replit project.

### **Overview of the Workflow**
1.  **User** finishes the Intake Form $\rightarrow$ Redirects to the **Full Application**.
2.  **User** fills out the Full Application (SSN, Bank Info, etc.).
3.  **Replit Server** saves this data to the database.
4.  **Replit Server** generates a unique, secure link: `https://your-app.replit.app/agent/application/[ID]`.
5.  **Replit Server** sends this data + the unique link to **GoHighLevel**.
6.  **Agent** receives an email from GHL with the link.
7.  **Agent** clicks the link $\rightarrow$ Sees a formatted page $\rightarrow$ Clicks "Download PDF".

### **Summary of What We Just Built**

1.  **User Flow:** Intake Form $\rightarrow$ Redirect to `/application` $\rightarrow$ Fills Full App $\rightarrow$ Success.
2.  **Backend:** Replit receives data, saves to DB, generates unique URL (`.../agent/application/123`), sends URL to GHL.
3.  **Agent Flow:** Agent gets GHL email $\rightarrow$ Clicks link $\rightarrow$ Sees `ApplicationView.html` populated with data $\rightarrow$ Clicks "Download PDF".
